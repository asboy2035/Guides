## Features
- #### Customizable Reminders
  Choose your notification style to be more or less obtrusive and modify the sound too.
- #### Fun Challenges
  Want to spice things up? Stand can suggest mini challenges like pushups, stretches, and more.
- #### Floating Widget
  Keep an eye on your next stand break with a minimal widget that floats on your desktop.
- #### Menu Bar Integration
  Stay in control at a glance with a simple, lightweight menu bar item.
- #### Dynamic Dock Icon
  Your Dock icon updates dynamically based on the current interval, giving you another stylish way to track your stand time!
- #### Non-Computer Mode
  Need a break that really pulls you away? Enter fullscreen mode to block distractions and truly focus on your current task.
