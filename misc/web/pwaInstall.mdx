Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) provide a native app-like experience across devices.
Here's how to install them on various platforms and browsers:

### Safari <mark>iOS</mark>
1. Open the PWA in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** icon at the bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Customize the name if you like, then tap **Add**.

### Safari <mark>macOS</mark>
1. Open the PWA in Safari.
2. Go to the **File** menu in the menu bar.
3. Click **Add to Dock**.
4. A new app icon will appear in your Dock and Launchpad.

### Chrome <mark>Android</mark>
1. Open the PWA in Chrome.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** in the top-right corner.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**.
4. Confirm the installation.

### Firefox <mark>Android</mark>
1. Open the PWA in Firefox.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu**.
3. Tap **Install** or **Add to Home screen**.
4. Follow the prompts to complete the installation.
